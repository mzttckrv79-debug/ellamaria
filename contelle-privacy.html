Contelle Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

Contelle respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

Contelle does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Local Storage

All content created inside Contelle is stored locally on your device. This may include planning notes, captions, ideas, schedules, and other content you create in the app.

This information does not leave your device unless you choose to share, export, or transfer it yourself.

Accounts

Contelle does not require user accounts, login credentials, names, email addresses, or passwords.

Analytics and Advertising

Contelle does not use analytics services and does not display third-party advertising.

Third-Party Services

Contelle does not transmit personal information to third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Contelle does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact: support@contelle.app
